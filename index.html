My Website Style Guide

Colors

Soft pink

#E87394

This is a vibrant pink with a touch of warmth, making it appear inviting and playful. It has a youthful and feminine feel, suitable for designs that aim to be soft yet energetic.

Deep magenta

#B04286

This shade is a rich magenta, deep and bold, with a hint of purple. It exudes sophistication and creativity, making it a great choice for vibrant designs that need to make a statement.

Strong teal

#01A575

A vivid and deep teal that combines the calming properties of blue with the natural freshness of green. This color is dynamic and balanced, ideal for spaces and designs that aim to inspire tranquility yet maintain a sense of energy.

Warm orange

#FBAC41

A bright and cheerful orange that radiates warmth and enthusiasm. This shade is very engaging and can be used to catch the eye or highlight the most important elements in a design. It's perfect for creative, playful, and energetic themes.

Dark navy

#0A355C

This is a dark navy blue, almost bordering on midnight blue. It's a very serious and professional shade that conveys trust and reliability. It's perfect for more formal or corporate designs where you want to communicate strength and dependability.
